Making History

    • 1923:

    • Breaking Ground

      Colleagues Marion Berry (M.B.) Turtle and Bill Hughes, join forces to open Turtle & Hughes at 446 Canal Street in New York City, an electrical equipment distributor to local businesses.

    • 1942:

    • Trailblazing

      Following the death of Bill Hughes in 1935, M.B. Turtle leads the company until 1942. His widow, Ethel Macnamara Turtle, manages our growth for 22 years; the first of three generations of women leaders.

    • 2018:

    • 95th Anniversary

      Growing to 900 employee-owners in locations across the U.S. and in Canada, Mexico and Puerto Rico, we continuously look to the future by expanding our electrical supply chain offerings to the customers we value.
